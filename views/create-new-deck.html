Getting Started

First begin by creating your very first deck!

Start by deciding a name to identify your collection of flashcards. This name will show up in your list of available decks you can view to study from, accessed by clicking the Current Decks link at the top of the page, so make it something intuitive! You can make as many decks as you wish, with as many or few questions as you need.

Each question entered requires an answer to be provided as well. Click on the add button below the question and answer forms to generate additional fields for adding new question/answer pairs. When you have finished, click the submit button found at the lower right to save your entire deck. Your deck will then be accessible to study from, or edit if you decide to add more flashcards to your deck, remove flashcards, or modify flashcards to better suit your studying needs.
